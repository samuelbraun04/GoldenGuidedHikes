Information about Hikes

General

  • Hikes organized year-round, showcasing nature's beauty in different seasons.
  • All hikes start at 9 AM (Local Time) from designated starting point. Arrive on time.
  • Hikes categorized as Easy, Moderate, or Difficult to suit various fitness levels.
  • Maximum capacity of 32 participants per hike for a comfortable group experience.
  • Experienced guides lead every hike, providing insights and ensuring safety.
  • Carefully selected routes and trails offer breathtaking views and memorable experiences.

Health and Safety

  • Bring your own hiking gear and essentials; equipment not provided.
  • Guides equipped with first aid kits and satellite phones for emergencies.
  • Carry your own water, snacks, and personal necessities for the hike.
  • Wear suitable footwear and clothing for comfort and safety.
  • Helicopter transport available for emergencies.
  • Guides trained to ensure a safe and enjoyable hiking experience.

Weather and Trail Conditions

  • Check the weather forecast before heading out and dress accordingly.
  • Be aware of any trail closures or warnings due to weather conditions.
  • Plan for sudden weather changes, especially in mountainous or remote areas.
  • Consider the trail's elevation gain and difficulty level when assessing weather conditions.
  • Research recent trail reports for information on obstacles like fallen trees or rockslides.

"Leave No Trace"

  • Follow the principles of Leave No Trace to minimize your impact on the environment.
  • Pack out all your trash, including food wrappers and tissues.
  • Stay on designated trails and avoid damaging vegetation or wildlife habitats.
  • Respect wildlife by observing from a distance and not feeding or disturbing them.
  • Minimize campfire impact by using established fire rings or stoves and properly extinguishing fires.
  • Use biodegradable or environmentally friendly toiletries and detergents.
  • Be considerate of other hikers by keeping noise levels down and yielding to uphill hikers when necessary.
